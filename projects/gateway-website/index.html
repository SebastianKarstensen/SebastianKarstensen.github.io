Gateway Website

Gateway (now known as “ITU Connect”) is a student organization with the goal of bringing various entertaining events to the students of the IT-University. Their previous website was outdated and difficult to maintain, so I build a new one. The website was made with fullPage.js and using Google Sheets as the back end.
